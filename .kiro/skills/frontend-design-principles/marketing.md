# Marketing Design

Guidance for landing pages, marketing sites, promotional materials, and creative artifacts. The philosophy: **bold intentionality that makes an impression.** Users encounter these once — make it memorable.

## Commit to a Direction
Pick a bold aesthetic and execute with conviction: Brutally Minimal · Maximalist · **Luxury & Refined** · Lo-Fi/Zine · Dark & Moody · Soft & Pastel · Editorial/Magazine · Retro-Futuristic · Handcrafted · Art Deco. (ZeroUpload = "Quiet Luxury" refined minimal — keep it.) The final design should feel singular.

## Typography as Voice
- Display type expressive, even risky. The headline font defines the aesthetic.
- Body legible but refined.
- Never default (skip Arial, Inter, Roboto, system fonts for display).
- Use the full range: dramatic size contrast, weight, case, letter-spacing.

## Color That Commits
Take a position: bold/saturated, moody/restrained, high-contrast minimal, or unexpected. Lead with a dominant color; punctuate with sharp accents. Avoid timid distributions.

## Spatial Composition
Asymmetry, overlap/z-depth, diagonal flow, grid-breaking elements, dramatic scale jumps, full-bleed moments, committed density OR generous negative space.

## Backgrounds & Atmosphere
Gradient meshes, noise/grain, geometric patterns, layered transparency, dramatic light, parallax, clip-paths, print textures, knockout type. Serve the direction, not demos.

## Motion for Impact
High-impact entrances (choreographed staggered reveals), scroll-triggered reveals, surprising hover states, cursor effects. Use Motion (Framer Motion) for React; push CSS far for vanilla. Respect reduced-motion.

## The Anti-Slop Checklist
Avoid: overused fonts (Inter/Roboto/Space Grotesk), clichéd palettes (purple-on-white, teal/coral, startup blue), predictable hero→features→testimonials→CTA, stock-photo energy, decorative blobs, "modern" without meaning. Ask: "Would I remember this tomorrow?"

## The Standard
Someone should describe it from memory. Bold maximalism and refined minimalism both work — the key is intentionality. Commit and execute.
